About Us

Who we are

Govt. polytechnic College Shopian was established in the year 2012. The college started functioning, from rented accommodation, in Khan Complex, Aliyalpora, Shopian. It is located at a distance of 50 kms from Srinagar. The college is situated on a link road leading to Historic Mughal Road. The college comprises of two buildings- An Academic Block for conducting classes and an Administrative Block. The college presently offers 3 years diploma in Electronics & Communication Engineering and Instrumentation & Control with an intake of 60 students in each branch. The college has a faculty of well experienced and highly qualified teachers. The Electronics and Communication Engineering Department is well equipped with basic infrastructure essential for making the students well acquainted with the instruments used in Electronics and Communication Engineering field. The department of E&C houses an Analog Electronics Lab, Microprocessor Lab, Communication Lab, Computer Lab and a Digital electronics Lab. The Instrumentation & Control department is in infancy stage and efforts are being made for its full development.

With best efforts from District Administration Shopian and J&K Govt. The land for the construction of College campus has been identified and construction work is going on at a speedy rate. The New Campus site is just 5kms away from the present location and we are hopeful of moving into it very soon.
