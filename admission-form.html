Admissions

Admission Process

The students are admitted to this college through entrance test conducted by BOPEE for Three year diploma in two discipline of engineering viz. Electronics & Communication Engineering and Instrumentation & Control Engineering. The application forms have to be submitted online through BOPEE website www.jakbopee.org or www.jakbopee.net in the month of Feburary every year and the entrance test is conducted tentatively in the month of April every year. For any query related to admission desirous persons can contact us through official telephone number or Email id provided in the “Contact Us” section of this website.

Admission Fee

The Admission fee is fixed by the Government and revised from time to time. At present, the following fee structure is in vogue:
1stYear(1st Semester) = Rs.2130/-
2nd Year(3rd Semester) = Rs.1930/-
3rd Year(5th Semester) = Rs.1930/-
Note: The 2nd Year and 3rd Year fee should be deposited at the beginning of 3rd and 5th Semester respectively. Fee once paid is not refundable under any circumstances except Rs.200/- (Library Security Deposit).

Admission Criteria

A Candidate who is desirous of seeking admission in the three year Diploma programme in any of the disciplines should :-

  • Be permanent resident of J&K State.
  • Have passed the Secondary School Examination/ Class 10th(Matriculation) or equivalent from a recognised board/ institution.
  • Have obtained 40 % marks in 10th class in case of open merit and 37% in case of reserved category.



List of Documents required at the time of admission:

  • 10th standard marks sheet.
  • Date of Birth Certificate/ Diploma Certificate.
  • Permanent Resident Certificate.
  • Category Certificate, if any.
  • Provisional Certificate/School Leaving Certificate.
  • Character Certificate from the Head of the institution last attended.
  • Four recent colour passport size photograph.
  • Admit Card.
  • Anti Ragging Affidavit in prescribed format.
  • Undertaking Affidavit in prescribed format.
  • Gap Certificate if applicable in prescribed format.
