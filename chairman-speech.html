Principal's Message

Welcome to the Government Polytechnic College Shopian

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome all the aspiring students to this institute. At GPCS, the students are imbibed with the skills of project based learning , a practical learning process, which helps the students to develop effective problem solving skills, self-directed learning, effective collaboration skills and intrinsic motivation. The students are encouraged towards effective goal setting from the time they join the engineering program which motivates them towards shaping their career. In this modern era of technology, we at GPC Shopian, put in our sincere efforts to keep pace with the rising demands of technology. our college stands for excellence, perfection and discipline. Our major focus and our concern are our students and our institution strives to impart the best knowledge for promoting and pursuing multidisciplinary engineering. We have a vision to develop a knowledge based society with clarity of thought and edge of excellence in the endowment of teaching and learning process. Our focus is on imparting holistic education, develop a community of intellectuals with talent and expertise that will participate in engineering excellence and national development through intellectual inputs and transformational activity centered experimental self-discovering process to embolden them, to assume responsibility, to take charge and act rightly and effectively. The faculty members here, are experts at integrating knowledge with competitive insights and practical applications. Their experience is drawn from their superior academic backgrounds. The diversity of our faculty and the supportive teaching-learning environment results into a unique, valuable and powerful learning experience.
In all our efforts, we seek the Lord's Grace to keep us on the path of virtue, courage and wisdom. May the grace and blessings flow through us to the world around us in all our efforts to make better quality engineers, who can make a positive impact in our society and help build our nation in these ever changing and challenging times. I welcome all the aspiring engineers in choosing this college so as to fulfil their dream to attain heights of glory and wish them all the best in their career
